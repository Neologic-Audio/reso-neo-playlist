Meet the Team

These are the minds behind enhancing the experience of Resonate users by allowing them to search for the most popular tracks for each tag/genre. This search algorithm helps users to discover new songs and improve the variety in a customized playlist. Users will be able to build a custom playlist using these recommendations tailored towards their taste in similar tracks.

This application will be on an easy-to-use and visually appealing site that will be available to users either locally through our GitHub repository
