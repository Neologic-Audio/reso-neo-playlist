Sign Up FAQ

When do I pay for music at Resonate?

All accounts come with .128 listening credits on the house -- explore up to 64 songs! When these credits are spent, you can purchase more credits via the Resonate Player. Credits are spent from your account automatically as you stream2own on the Resonate Player.

Do I automatically become a member of the co-op when I create a Listener Account?

No, a Listener Account is not a co-op membership in itself. For artists and labels, uploading music earns membership to the co-op. For listeners, co-op membership is earned with an annual â‚¬5 contribution to the co-op.

I already have a Listener Account. How do I become a member?

You can become a Listener Member here.

Can I manage multiple artists, bands or personas from a single Artist Account?

No, you can not manage multiple artist profiles from a single Artist Account at this time. This is planned as a future feature.

As a small label of a few artists, do I have to wait for Label Account signup to be re-enabled? Can I sign up our artists now by creating and managing multiple Artist Accounts?

You may set up individual Artist Accounts for each artist and manage those accounts directly. When we re-enable Label signup, we will at that time be able to associate your artists with your new Label Account.

Can I change my account type later?

Yes. To change an existing Listener Account to an Artist Account, visit this page. For changes between other account types, contact members@resonate.is. Note, changing an account type will not automatically change a previously registered membership.

What do members do at Resonate?

Our members are building a music economy based on fairness, transparency, and community. They govern our decisions, development and share in our material success. Nurturing and developing our platform; members take on many different roles from making music to technology, to business strategy, communication and policy, to the co-op and COMMONS.

I don't want another streaming service at the moment but want to help Resonate grow. How can I help?

We welcome all support and donations!
